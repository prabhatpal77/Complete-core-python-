Operators:-
          Operators are nothing but construct/ syntax, which are use to perform the operation under operants.
          Python supports different verities of operators.
          1. Arithmatic operator.
          2. Comparison operator.
          3. Logical operator.
          4. Bitwise operator.
          5. Assignment operator.
          6. Special operator.
          7. Membership operator.
          8. Identity operator.
